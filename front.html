the concept

What is Influenza?

Not the sick kind. The kind that spreads a product from one feed to the next until everyone's talking about it — on purpose, with a paper trail.

Brands don't need more reach. They need the right reach — creators whose audience already cares about what they're selling. Influenza reads your category, your niche, your numbers, and matches you before you even open a DM.

Creators don't need more followers. They need partners who pay on time and campaigns that actually fit their page. So we flipped the cold-pitch model — brands come to you with a brief, not a follow request.

Think of it as patient zero for good collabs. One match, and it spreads.
